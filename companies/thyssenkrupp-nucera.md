## 1. Business overview

thyssenkrupp nucera AG & Co. KGaA is a global provider of electrolysis
technologies headquartered in Dortmund, Germany. The company operates
two segments: Chlor-Alkali (CA), supplying industrial plants for
chlorine and caustic soda production, and Green Hydrogen (gH2),
delivering alkaline water electrolysis (AWE) for green hydrogen
production. The company holds over 60 years of chlor-alkali heritage
and is expanding into clean hydrogen infrastructure.

The Chlor-Alkali segment is a mature, profitable business serving
chemical manufacturers globally. The Green Hydrogen segment
manufactures scalable electrolysis equipment targeting industrial
end-uses, synthetic fuels, and green steel. Major projects include
700+ MW for H2 Green Steel in Sweden (operations targeted end-2025
with ramp-up in 2026) and 400+ MW at NEOM's Saudi Arabian green
hydrogen facility.

The company manufactures in multiple technology platforms: alkali
electrolyser (scalum), bipolar membrane technology (BM2.7 v7 and
e-BiTAC v7 achieving <1,960 kWh/t NaOH), high-temperature SOEC (pilot
with Fraunhofer IKTS), and high-pressure alkaline electrolysis (IP
acquired from Green Hydrogen Systems). As of FY2024/25, the company
has deployed over 3 GW of electrolyser capacity across 600+ projects.

## 5. Hydrogen-specific operational KPIs

- **Installed / shipped capacity:** 3+ GW electrolyser capacity deployed
  globally across 600+ projects. H2 Green Steel: 700+ MW; NEOM 400+ MW
  installed with >4 million safe working hours. Q1 FY2025/26 gH2 sales
  down 50% YoY to €77m.
- **Order backlog:** €0.6bn at 30 Sept 2025; €489m at 31 Dec 2025.
- **Book-to-bill ratio (FY2024/25):** Order intake €348m / Revenue €845m
  = 0.41x.
- **Manufacturing capacity:** Pilot high-temperature SOEC plant
  (Fraunhofer IKTS partnership) commenced Q1 FY2025/26; main
  manufacturing in Germany and Denmark.
- **Announced large contracts (past 12 months):** Cepsa 300 MW
  reservation agreement (Spain); largest Chlor-Alkali new-build in
  company history from Middle East customer (Dec 2025, high
  double-digit million EUR); 260 MW FEED study in India (Mar 2026);
  Chlorum Solutions Casa Grande, Arizona.
