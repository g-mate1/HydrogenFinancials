## 1. Business overview

Plug Power Inc. is a leading provider of hydrogen fuel cell systems
and green hydrogen generation technology, having deployed more than
74,000 fuel cell units and over 280 fuelling stations worldwide. The
company operates along the entire hydrogen value chain: production
(GenEco electrolyser systems), storage and distribution (cryogenic
equipment, hydrogen fuel), and end-use applications (GenDrive material
handling, GenSure backup power for stationary applications, and
emerging GenFreezer liquid hydrogen cooling).

The Material Handling business segment serves warehouse and logistics
operators globally, with GenDrive fuel cell systems deployed at
enterprise customers across North America, Europe, and Asia. The
GenSure stationary backup power systems (installed nameplate capacity
over 8 MW) serve telecommunications, critical infrastructure, and
data-centre applications across 35 countries. More recently, Plug
Power expanded into green hydrogen production through its GenEco
electrolyser platform (1–40 MW per unit), with over 300 MW shipped
globally across six continents as of end-2025. Revenue sources now
include electrolyser systems, hydrogen fuel sales, cryogenic
equipment, and legacy fuel cell product lines.

Plug Power holds the world's largest installed base of commercial
hydrogen fuel cells, with over 1 billion cumulative operating hours in
commercial deployment. The company is pursuing major European
electrolyser opportunities, with recent wins including the Baie-Comeau
Hy2gen project in Quebec (275 MW FEED contract, April 2026), the GALP
Sines Refinery in Portugal (100 MW), and NASA's first liquid hydrogen
supply contract.
