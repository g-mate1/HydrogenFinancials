## 1. Business overview

SFC Energy AG is a German-listed pure-play developer and
manufacturer of portable hydrogen and methanol fuel cells for
stationary and mobile power generation. Customers include NATO
militaries, telecoms / tower operators, industrial automation,
oil & gas producers, and off-grid remote-site operators. Core
product lines: EFOY Pro methanol fuel cells (up to 500 W);
EMILY (NATO-stocked military methanol); JENNY (portable hydrogen
for soldiers); PEM hydrogen fuel cells 2.5–50 kW. The company has
shipped 86,000+ fuel cells since inception and opened a Salt Lake
City, Utah manufacturing facility in Q4 2025. SFC is one of the
few hydrogen pure-plays with positive adjusted EBITDA.

## 10. Segments

- **Clean Energy & Mobility:** ~50% of FY2025 revenue (H2 + methanol
  FCs — EFOY Pro, EMILY, JENNY, PEM hydrogen). Exposed to Indian
  defence order delays and USD/INR currency headwinds.
- **Oil & Gas:** ~22% of FY2025 revenue; legacy power management
  for upstream customers; softness from weaker exploration capex.
- **Corporate / other:** ~28% unallocated.

## 11. Key risks

- **Defence-order volatility:** ~50% of revenue concentrated in
  defence / security; large orders often rephased.
- **Methanol vs hydrogen product mix** transition risk.
- **Indian JV dependency** via FC TecNrgy.
- **Unhedged FX exposure** (USD, INR, CAD) affects gross margin.
- **Backlog decline** (−25% YoY) reduces revenue visibility.
- **PEM H2 competition** from Plug Power, Ballard, ITM etc.
