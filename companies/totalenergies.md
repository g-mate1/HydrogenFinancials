## 1. Business overview

TotalEnergies SE is a globally integrated energy company with
operations in upstream oil & gas, LNG, renewables & power, biofuels,
SAF and hydrogen. The company operates 10 European refineries, each
consuming ~50 kt/year of hydrogen. Its stated 2030 target is to
supply ~500 kt/year of green hydrogen to European refineries,
replacing grey H2 and equivalent to ~3 GW of electrolyser capacity.
TotalEnergies is primarily an offtaker / customer in hydrogen rather
than an OEM — it relies on partners (Air Liquide, Air Products,
RWE, Engie, Iberdrola, Masdar) to supply H2 under long-term offtake.
~€10bn of new-energies capex through 2030 underpins the strategy.

## 5. Hydrogen-specific operational KPIs

- **Current demand:** ~500 kt/year across 10 European refineries
  (largely grey H2 today).
- **2030 green H2 target:** up to 500 kt/year via partner supply,
  equivalent to ~3 GW of electrolyser capacity.
- **Announced offtake contracts (past 12–24 months):** Air Liquide
  70 kt/yr by 2030 (Normandy, Antwerp, Grandpuits, La Mède); RWE
  30 kt/yr (Leuna, 300 MW, 2030); Air Products 70 kt/yr; OranjeWind
  JV (TTE + Air Liquide 50/50; 45 kt/yr, 250 MW, 2029); Masshylia
  (with Engie, 10 kt/yr, 20 MW at La Mède, 2029).
- **Supply security (March 2025 disclosure):** Feyzin 0% secured,
  Donges ~10%, Normandy ~10%, Antwerp ~15%, Grandpuits ~95%, La
  Mède 35%.

## 11. Key hydrogen risks

- **Refinery decarbonisation economics:** green H2 >2–3x more
  expensive than grey H2 without strong carbon pricing support.
- **Partner concentration & JV attrition:** historical precedent of
  partner withdrawal (Engie paused capex 2024–25).
- **Customer / refinery demand erosion:** Western European refining
  throughput declining structurally.
- **Electricity sourcing for 3 GW:** dedicated renewable PPAs and
  grid constraints; OranjeWind partially hedges.
- **Regulatory / subsidy cliff:** IPCEI and CEF funding continuity
  and EU RFNBO definition changes.
- **Electrolyser technology and supply-chain inflation** affects
  project capex and timelines.
