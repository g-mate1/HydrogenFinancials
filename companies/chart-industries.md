**Headquarters:** Ball Ground, Georgia, USA
**Listing:** NYSE, GTLS
**Reporting currency:** USD
**Fiscal year end:** 31 December
**Segment:** Cryogenic equipment and gas-processing technology
spanning hydrogen liquefaction, storage, distribution, carbon
capture, LNG and industrial gases.
**Data last updated:** 2026-04-13
**Latest full fiscal year reported:** FY2025
**Latest quarter reported:** Q4 FY2025 (27 February 2026)

## 1. Business overview

Chart Industries is a global manufacturer of highly engineered
cryogenic equipment with material exposure to hydrogen across the
value chain. It has deployed 800+ hydrogen tanks globally (aerospace,
FCEV fuelling, industrial applications) and supplies hydrogen
liquefaction plants (5–150 t/day), LH2 storage tanks, hydrogen
fuelling stations, LH2 trailers, and compressors / blowers via its
Howden subsidiary (acquired 2023). The Sustainable Energy Solutions
(SES) unit provides post-combustion Cryogenic Carbon Capture™.
**Major corporate activity:** the Chart–Flowserve $19bn all-stock
merger closed in February 2026 (after Flowserve briefly terminated
in July 2025), and **Baker Hughes announced acquisition of Chart
for $13.6bn EV ($210/share cash)** on 29 July 2025; shareholder
approval obtained October 2025; expected close Q2 2026 subject to
regulatory review.

## 10. Hydrogen & CCS product / service mix

- **Hydrogen liquefaction plants** (5–150 t/day) including
  compressors, cold boxes, storage and loading systems.
- **LH2 storage tanks (Type 2/3)** for FCEV, aerospace,
  liquefaction; 800+ deployed.
- **Hydrogen fuelling stations** (integrated packages).
- **LH2 trailers and ISO containers**.
- **Howden compressors / blowers** for electrolyser and industrial
  use.
- **Sustainable Energy Solutions (SES):** Cryogenic Carbon Capture™
  + Earthly Labs distributed CCS.
- **Service, retrofit and spares:** RSL segment 30.6% of FY2025
  consolidated sales (~$1.31bn).
