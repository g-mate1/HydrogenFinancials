## 1. Business overview

Atome plc is a pre-revenue AIM-listed project developer creating
green hydrogen, green ammonia and green fertiliser facilities
powered by 100% renewable electricity. The company was demerged
from President Energy plc in December 2021. Primary focus is
**Paraguay**, where Atome is developing the **145 MW Villeta
project** — its flagship — targeting 260 kt/yr of calcium ammonium
nitrate (CAN) green fertiliser commencing in 2028. Villeta combines
renewable hydroelectric power from the Itaipu Dam, alkaline
electrolysis, Yara International 100% offtake, and an EPC contract
from Casale. A secondary focus is **Iceland** (green H2 / ammonia
via geothermal); a joint venture with Cavendish S.A. (Costa Rica)
pursues Central America opportunities.

## 5. Hydrogen-specific operational KPIs

- **Installed / operational capacity:** zero. Villeta project in
  FEED / pre-FID; 145 MW alkaline electrolyser; 260 kt/yr green
  fertiliser target output.
- **Announced capital packages:** $465m fixed-price EPC contract
  with Casale (March 2025); $115m Heads of Terms anchor equity
  from Hy24 (February 2025); $240m debt framework; $50m Green
  Climate Fund concessional funding commitment.
- **Offtake:** Yara International 10-year 100% offtake agreement
  (signed pre-2024, reaffirmed).
- **Other projects:** Iceland green-H2 / ammonia (early
  feasibility); Costa Rica NAC JV (framework agreement with ICE).

## 6. Balance sheet and liquidity

- **Cash runway:** ~2–4 months at H1 2025 burn (~USD 3.1m per
  quarter). Dependent on Peter Levine (chairman) shareholder loan
  facility of up to £4m available until 30 September 2026.
- **Most recent capital raise:** September 2024 placing; total
  2024 raised $6.3m gross ($3.7m cash-in by 31 Dec 2024). Further
  placings executed in H1 2025 (amounts not disclosed).
- **Going-concern language:** **Yes — material uncertainty**
  disclosed in the 2024 annual report; dependent on further raises,
  project financing completion and the Peter Levine facility.
- **FID timeline:** Targeted end-February 2026 after multiple
  revisions (mid-2025 → end-H2 2025 → end-2025 → Jan 2026 → Feb
  2026).
