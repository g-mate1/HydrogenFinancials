## 1. Business overview

Nel ASA is a global manufacturer of alkaline and PEM water electrolysers
that split water into hydrogen and oxygen using renewable electricity.
Customers include industrial end-users seeking green hydrogen for
ammonia synthesis, steel production, petroleum refining, methanol
manufacturing and chemical processing. Nel's customer base spans more
than 80 countries, with notable contracts across Europe (Spain, Norway,
Germany), North America (Mississippi hydrogen hub), and partnerships in
India.

Nel operates a dual-technology strategy: alkaline (lower cost, proven
at scale) and PEM (higher efficiency, compact footprint).
Manufacturing capacity totals 1.5 GW annually (1.0 GW alkaline at
Herøya; 0.5 GW PEM at Wallingford, Connecticut). The company positions
itself in hydrogen *production*, supplying equipment and systems to
project developers and industrial operators. In June 2024 Nel completed
a spin-off of its hydrogen fuelling division (now Cavendish Hydrogen
ASA), allowing renewed focus on electrolyser manufacturing.
