## 1. Business overview

Cummins Inc. is a global manufacturer of diesel, natural-gas and
alternative-power systems for heavy-duty vehicles, stationary power
and industrial applications. Accelera was formed in 2021 by
rebranding the New Power Business and integrating Hydrogenics
(acquired 2019 for ~USD 290m; Cummins bought out Air Liquide's
residual 19% stake in 2023). Accelera produces PEM and alkaline
electrolysers, fuel-cell powertrains, battery systems and eAxles.
In **February 2026 Cummins announced an exit from new
electrolyser sales** following a USD 458m FY2025 impairment charge
(USD 210m goodwill + USD 30m inventory write-down + USD 218m Q4
charges); it will honour existing customer commitments only (BP
Lingen 100 MW, Linde Niagara 35 MW, Varennes 90 MW, Tyczka 5 MW).
Accelera continues developing battery-electric and fuel-cell
platforms for commercial vehicles via the Isuzu / Daimler / Paccar
partnerships.

## 5. Hydrogen-specific operational KPIs (Accelera)

- **Committed / delivered capacity:** 35 MW PEM delivered to Linde
  Niagara Falls (Sep 2025); 100 MW PEM in production for BP Lingen
  (commissioning 2027); 90 MW PEM for Varennes Carbon Recycling
  (Shell/Suncor/Proman consortium); 5 MW to Tyczka GmbH.
- **Manufacturing capacity:** Guadalajara, Spain electrolyser factory
  (opened April 2024, 500 MW/year scalable to 1 GW); Oevel, Belgium
  (~1 GW/year equivalent; workforce cuts following Feb 2026 exit).
- **Strategic exit:** New electrolyser sales halted Feb 2026;
  existing backlog (~USD 500m prior to exit) to be delivered through
  2027.

## 11. Key hydrogen risks

- **Strategic exit from electrolyser sales** removes growth
  optionality; residual backlog execution risk.
- **Impairment & losses** reflect green-H2 demand deterioration
  and Treasury 45V rule changes.
- **Customer project delays** (BP Lingen 2027, Varennes FID).
- **Electrolyser cost parity** vs alkaline competition.
- **Core engine transition risk** as diesel regulation tightens.
- **Competitive / supply-chain pressures** at Guadalajara /
  Oevel facilities.
